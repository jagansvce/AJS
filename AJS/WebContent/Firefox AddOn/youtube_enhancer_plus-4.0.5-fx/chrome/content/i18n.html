How to translate YouTube Enhancer Plus

Copy everything below the grey line and send it back to webmaster [at] mrfdev.com after having translated the words and sentences marked in blue. You don't need to translate the rest, it's just used to remind me where are placed the words and sentences.

Please respect as much as you can the punctuation marks. For example, when there is no trailing period, don't add one, and vice versa.
%S, %1$S, and %2$S are placeholders used by the add-on to insert text that is generated dynamically, don't remove them.

Before you start the translation, to avoid you unnecessary work you should contact me to let me know in which language you want to translate the add-on, I will tell you if it has already been translated in this language, or if someone else has already proposed to translate it. Thank you!
